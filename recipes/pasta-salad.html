Pasta Salad - Explosion of Flavor

In three simple steps, you will be able to get the explosion of flavor into your mouth.
Are you ready to be sent to flavor heaven? Let the fun begin....with some ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 pound tri-colored spiral pasta
  • 6 tablespoons salad seasoning mix
  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • Â½ yellow bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 (2.25 ounce) can black olives, chopped

Directions

  1. In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook pasta until al dente, rinse under cold water and drain.

  2. Whisk together the salad spice mix and Italian dressing.

  3. In a salad bowl, combine the pasta, cherry tomatoes, bell peppers and olives. Pour dressing over salad; toss and refrigerate overnight.
